The Office

The Office is an American television sitcom broadcast on NBC.

The show was created as an adaptation of the British series of the same name.

It is a mockumentary that follows the day-to-day lives of the employees of the Scranton, Pennsylvania branch of Dunder Mifflin, a fictional paper supply company.

The series ran on NBC in the United States from March 24, 2005 to May 16, 2013.

The series consists of 9 seasons.
